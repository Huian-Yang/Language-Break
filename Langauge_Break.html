Language Break

About

Description: Hello and welcome to Language Break! This website is dedicated to promoting resources and applications that aid the language learning process. We believe that there is still a huge language barrier in the world, and we want to change that! Whether you are someone currently struggling with the language barrier in your country or you are someone who is interested in learning a new language, this is the right place for you!
